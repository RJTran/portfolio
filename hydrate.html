HYDRATE

Hydrate is a conceptual mobile app to help keep track of daily fluid intake by creating goals, reminders, and stats.

TOOLS

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Sketch

The Problem

Living in California, I can see people can sometimes struggle with drinking the proper amount of fluids to stay hydrated especially while staying busy with work or other activites.

After looking at other hydration apps, I’ve noticed a lot of them did not have a very modern UI that is user friendly. They also contained a lot of other features that made the app feel a bit too excessive. I’ve designed this app to look and feel good while keeping it simple when tracking fluid intake.

Style Guide

I wanted to stick with a playful design with heavily contrasted colors. I believed that having this choice of style guide would allow the users to feel more engaged and happy with using the app, and overall be excited to stay hydrated.
